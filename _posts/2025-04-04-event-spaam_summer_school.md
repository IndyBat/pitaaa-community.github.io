 The SPAAM Community is happy to announce that it will offer it’s fourth (virtual) summer school course sponsored by the [Werner Siemens Foundation](https://www.wernersiemens-stiftung.ch/) and [NFDI4Microbiota](https://nfdi4microbiota.de/)!

👩🏾‍🎓 This course is aimed at masters students and early-stage PhD students and will take place online on **4th - 8th of August 2025**.

📅  Applications are open from now until **May 30st 2025**. 

**To apply and learn more, visit the dedicated [website](https://www.spaam-community.org/wss-summer-school).**
