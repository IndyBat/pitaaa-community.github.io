We’re happy to announce SPAAM8 – a gathering of experts in ancient metagenomics research!
 
SPAAM8 will be taking place as a hybrid satellite meeting prior to the ICP 2026 in Stockholm!
 
## When?
22nd June 2026
 
## Where?
🇮🇸 This year, the in-person event will be hosted in Stockholm, in the same venue as ICP 2026.

👩‍💻 In case you can’t make it to Stockholm, there will be a possibility to attend online.
 

<!-- ## An overview of the SPAAM7 program can be found [here](/assets/media/Preliminary_program_SPAAM7.pdf) -->


## Abstract submission
Please, submit your abstract through [this form](https://forms.gle/psw9GS3ZyrLVPbbo7). 
**Deadline:** May 1st, 2026
